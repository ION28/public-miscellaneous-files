FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
What is penetration testing? Penetration testing is a proactive cybersecurity assessment that simulates real-world attacks to identify vulnerabilities in a company's systems, networks, or applications.
What is threat intelligence? Threat intelligence refers to the knowledge and insights gained about potential cyber threats and attackers. It helps organizations understand the tactics, techniques, and procedures used by threat actors to better protect their systems.
How do you conduct your architecture reviews? We do a mix of discussions and documentation reviews to help fully understand your unique setup then collaborate with you to develop opportunities to build upon your current posture.
Why should large Fortune 500 companies choose ICMP Industries? ICMP Industries has extensive experience working with large organizations and understands their unique cybersecurity challenges. Our team of experts provides tailored solutions and industry-leading services to help mitigate risks and protect valuable assets.
How can I contact ICMP Industries for consulting services? To inquire about our cybersecurity consulting services, you can reach out to us through our website's contact form or by emailing us at contact@icmpindustries.com. Our team will promptly respond to your inquiry.
